What is this thing?

Using a credit card is both convenient and rewarding, but it becomes less so when there's a transaction or "convenience" fee at the checkout.

"Worth It?" is a simple calculation tool that shows how much extra you'll earn or lose when there's a transaction fee involved. Simply enter in the numbers, and it'll tell you if your credit card's rewards outweigh the fee, or vice versa.
